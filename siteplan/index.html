Site Purpose:

This site is designed as a showcase for my photography content and as a place to contact me. The site needs to lay out the different services that I provide. These will be laid out with different price options. There needs to be a contact page where there is a way to email me and a link to my different socials. There also needs to be a page that allows me to sort and showcase all of the different work that I have done in the past. There needs to be two stages for the pictures, stage one is having them as a grid or flex view, the other needs to let the user click on one and blow it up to higher quality. There will be a left menu with all of the different albums that I can drop the pictures into.
